# Third-Party Reader Fonts

TreeTales may bundle reader fonts from the Core Collection in
`nicoverbruggen/ebook-fonts` for Reader Appearance controls.

When bundled fonts are added, removed, or updated:

- Record the bundled font family name.
- Link the upstream source.
- Record the license that applies to that font.
- Preserve required copyright and license notices.
- Bundle Regular, Italic, Bold, and Bold Italic styles for each reader font.
- Do not sell the font files by themselves.

Current bundled source:

- `public/fonts/reader/`
- Upstream release: https://github.com/nicoverbruggen/ebook-fonts/releases/tag/v2026.04.28
- Release asset: `other-core-fonts.zip`
