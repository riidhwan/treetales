# Third-Party Reader Fonts

These reader fonts are bundled from the `other-core-fonts.zip` release asset in
`nicoverbruggen/ebook-fonts` v2026.04.28.

Source: https://github.com/nicoverbruggen/ebook-fonts/releases/tag/v2026.04.28

Bundled families:

- Readerly
- Sourcerer
- Cartisse
- NV Charis
- NV Garamond
- NV Jost
- NV Bitter
- NV Legible Next
- NV Palatium

Most bundled families are distributed under the SIL Open Font License 1.1.
Cartisse is derived from Charter/XCharter and carries the Bitstream
Charter/XCharter-derived notice. Preserve the copyright and license metadata
embedded in the font files and keep project documentation current when these
assets are changed.
